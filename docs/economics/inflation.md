# Inflation

Inflation is the persistent increase in the general price of goods and services
in an economy leading the the constant drop of the currency's value.

## Causes of inflation

- Increase in demand.
- Increase in population.
- Increase in money supply. When the increase in supply of money outweights
  the output of good and services, it couses inflation.

## Types of inflation

### Demand pull inflation

When aggregate demand exceeds supply.

### Cost pull inflation

When the general price of goods and services increases due to the increase
in cose of factors of production(or simply increase in cost of production)

## Effects of inflation

- Reduces the purchasing power of money
- Increase in salary
- Decrease in interest rate

## fighting methods

- Fiscal policies
- Wage and price control

# Deflation

## Causes

- Increase in supply relative to aggregate demand.
- Decrease in purchasing power.

## Effects

- Increase in purchasing power and comsumption
